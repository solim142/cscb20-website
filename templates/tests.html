Study Advice

The term test is designed to reflect the work you've done in lectures, tutorials, and assignments. Regular class attendance and diligent work on assignments are key ways to prepare, as these activities reinforce your learning.

  1. Practice Writing Queries by Hand

    • You'll be required to write queries on paper without the support of an SQL editor or IDE.
    • Practice writing queries for all the operators we studied so far.
    • Check your queries carefully for syntax and logic errors. Get comfortable identifying issues without automatic error highlighting.
