in loving memory of jeremy the cat

jeremy jones

jeremy was a loving cat who always had his owner's back. one time when his owner dropped a churro on the ground, jeremy guarded his owner from getting diseases by eating the rest of the churro and biting his hand off so that he would never have to be exposed to the germs of the ground ever again. unfortunately after that incident, jeremy had to be put down and that was the end of jeremy
